Our Projects

CuBuild

CuBuild, an e-commerce website for 3D printing services. Users can upload their designs, receive a price quote for printing, and have the product delivered to their home. Additionally, users can purchase pre-printed products available on the platform. The website was developed using Next.js, Node.js (Express), MongoDB for the database, AWS for storage, and Cloudinary for image compression.

LOS Academy

LOS Academy is an online learning platform offering comprehensive Arabic language and Islamic knowledge courses through remote learning technology. Using Next.js, Node.js (Express), PostgreSQL, Sequelize, Socket.IO, and the Zoom Meetings API.

Elazl

Elazl company is a water and heat isolation company, featuring blogs and an admin panel. The technology stack includes Angular for the frontend, Supabase for the backend, PostgreSQL, and Supabase storage.
